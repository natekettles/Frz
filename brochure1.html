Established 8-Bedroom Lodge for Sale on 7.9ha Small Holding

NO TRANSFER DUTY NO TRANSFER SALE OF BUSINESS An exceptional opportunity to acquire a fully operational and well-equipped lodge situated on a 7.9-hectare small holding, offering both lifestyle appeal and strong income-generating potential. The property features eight spacious bedrooms, each with its own en-suite bathroom, ensuring comfort and privacy for guests. Lodge operations are supported by two fully functional kitchens, ideal for catering and staff use. The property is exceptionally well resourced, offering three boreholes, of which two are equipped and one is unequipped, complemented by a water treatment system and a 2x 5,000-litre water storage tank, ensuring reliable and sustainable water supply. Additional infrastructure includes a cold room and four separate storerooms, ideal for efficient lodge management and storage. Outdoor and guest amenities are well catered for, featuring an inviting outdoor braai area complete with a bread oven and pizza oven, a swimming pool, and two well-appointed external bathrooms for guest convenience. The property provides shaded parking for approximately 10 vehicles, ensuring ample and secure parking. Electricity supply is robust, supported by a 25kVA Eskom transformer as well as a generator, ensuring uninterrupted power during outages. The lodge is registered under an established business name, which may be transferred with the sale, and all furniture is included, making this a true turnkey investment ready for immediate operation. Set within expansive grounds, the small holding offers excellent scope for future expansion, additional accommodation units, or further hospitality development, subject to the necessary approvals. This outstanding lodge presents a rare opportunity for investors, hospitality operators, or lifestyle buyers seeking a fully furnished, operational property with excellent infrastructure in a tranquil rural setting.
